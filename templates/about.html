About DREAMLENS AI

DREAMLENS AI helps you explore the hidden meanings of dreams using interpretable methods and free, open models. We combine dataset-based matches, zero-shot thematic detection, and small generative models to provide fast, insightful interpretations without relying on paid APIs by default.

Our approach

  • Dataset matching: We use TF-IDF similarity against a crowdsourced dream-interpretations dataset for grounded, human-like matches.
  • Theme detection: Zero-shot classification finds emotional and situational themes in a dream (fear, loss, travel, relationships).
  • Generative suggestions: Flan-T5 produces short reflection prompts when a dataset match isn't confident.

Ideas & Next steps

  • Personal Journals: Let users keep private dream journals and track recurring themes over time.
  • Community Collections: Allow anonymized community-contributed dreams to improve dataset coverage and discover archetypes.
  • Guided Reflection: Offer short, therapist-inspired reflection exercises rather than definitive judgments.
  • Privacy First: Provide optional local-only mode so data never leaves the user's device or explicitly ask for consent to save annotations.
